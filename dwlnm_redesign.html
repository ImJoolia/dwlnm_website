Who We Are

Form & Function
in Equal Parts

DWL Architects & Planners, Inc. is a local architecture firm deeply rooted in the community of Albuquerque, New Mexico. We collaborate closely with artists, historians, and cultural experts to ensure our designs authentically reflect the essence of our vibrant state.

Every building has a purpose — our emphasis on intentional design sets us apart, considering both form and function while delivering architectural services that exceed expectations.

  • Useful and valuable — withstanding robust use and change over time
  • Inspiring spaces that energize those who inhabit them
  • Deeply rooted in the unique ecology and culture of New Mexico
  • Each project strengthens community through context and place
